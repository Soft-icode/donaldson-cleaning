About Us

Who We Are

DONALDSON HOME INDUSTRIAL CLEANING SERVICE is an indigenous company registered under the law of federal republic of Nigeria business Act. As the name implies, is a business set up to provide general industrial cleaning services to both public and private sector either onshore or offshore with high quality hygiene and safety approach.

Our Mission

We are committed to provide excellent and high quality industrial cleaning services at affordable price, quick service delivery through technology and manpower supply. On dexterity is committed to reduce environmental pollution and it enhance high level of hygiene and safety to the best international practice and standard with professional safety experts.
